## Recommended Verification Workflow

Before relying on any regulatory information retrieved through this server:

1. **Identify the relevant FSA guideline or inspection item** using this server's search tools
2. **Retrieve the official document** from https://www.fsa.go.jp/common/law/ (Japanese) or https://www.fsa.go.jp/en/refer/guide/ (English)
3. **Confirm the version and effective date** — FSA guidelines are updated regularly
4. **Read the Japanese version** if the matter is substantive — the English translation may lag
5. **Consult qualified legal or compliance counsel** for interpretation and application to your specific facts
